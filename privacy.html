Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Last updated: v1.0

1. Who We Are

Travelo (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides travel search and booking services. This policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our websites, apps, and services (“Services”).

2. Data We Collect

  • Account & Contact: name, email, phone.
  • Booking details: itineraries, passengers, preferences, payment tokens (processed by PCI-compliant providers).
  • Usage & device: pages viewed, clicks, IP, browser, OS, identifiers (cookies/SDKs).
  • Location: approximate geolocation derived from IP or with your consent.
Sources (expand)
  • Directly from you (forms, bookings, support).
  • Automatically via cookies, SDKs, and analytics.
  • Partners (airlines, hotels, payment providers) for fulfilling bookings and fraud prevention.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Services.
  • Process bookings, payments, refunds, and customer support.
  • Personalize content (saved searches, recommendations).
  • Security, fraud detection, debugging, and analytics.
  • Marketing & communications where permitted; you can opt out anytime.

4. Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

  • Contract – to deliver the Services you request.
  • Legitimate interests – security, analytics, product improvement.
  • Consent – cookies/marketing where required; you may withdraw at any time.
  • Legal obligation – comply with applicable laws.

5. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use cookies/SDKs for essential operations, analytics, and personalization. You can manage preferences via your browser and the banner below.

Tip: For a strict setup, block third-party cookies and enable “Do Not Track” in your browser.

6. Sharing Your Information

  • Vendors/Processors: hosting, analytics, payments, customer support.
  • Travel partners: airlines/hotels to fulfill bookings.
  • Legal & safety: to comply with law or protect rights.
  • Business transfers: merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

7. International Transfers

We may transfer data internationally using safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or other lawful mechanisms.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described or as required by law and accounting obligations.

9. Your Rights

  • Access, correction, deletion, and portability (where applicable).
  • Object to processing or restrict it in certain cases.
  • Withdraw consent without affecting prior lawful processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

10. Security

We apply administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No method is 100% secure; please use strong, unique passwords.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under the age required by local law. We do not knowingly collect data from such children.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be highlighted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions or requests, contact us at privacy@travelo.example.

14. Definitions

  • Personal data: any information relating to an identified or identifiable person.
  • Processing: any operation performed on personal data.
  • Controller/Processor: as defined by applicable data protection laws.
