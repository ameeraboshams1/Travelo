Refunds & Changes: How Travelo Keeps It Simple

Published Apr 2, 2025 • 6 min read

Plans change. Your booking rules shouldn’t be a maze. Here’s how to read fare rules, request changes, and understand refund timelines—without jargon.

1) Read the fare rules (the smart way)

Every ticket has rules set by the airline. On Travelo, we surface the essentials during checkout: change fees, refundability, and no-show penalties. Screenshot this summary for later.

2) Change vs. cancel

  • Change: Usually a change fee + any fare difference.
  • Cancel: Refund depends on your fare (refundable, partially refundable, or non-refundable).

3) Refund timelines

Approved refunds typically appear in 5–14 business days, depending on your payment method and bank processing times.

Heads-up: Some banks show a “pending reversal” before the final credit posts. That’s normal.

4) 24-hour cool-off

Some routes/regions allow a short grace period. If available for your fare, you’ll see it clearly at checkout and in your confirmation.

5) Best practice checklist

  • Start changes from My Bookings to avoid duplicate requests.
  • Pick alternative dates/times first—higher success rate.
  • Keep your original payment card active until the refund posts.
