Hotel Hacks: Upgrades, Late Check-outs, and Perks

Published May 5, 2025 • 9 min read

Hotels love happy, low-maintenance guests. With a few friendly asks and good timing, you can unlock extras without elite status.

1) Ask at the right moment

Arrive slightly after peak check-in (around 5–6 pm). More rooms are cleaned and released into inventory. That’s your chance to request a higher floor or quieter wing.

2) Mention the occasion

Birthdays, honeymoons, first-time visits—hotels note this. You’re not entitled to an upgrade, but you’ll be top of mind for small surprises.

3) Join the property’s free program

Even independent hotels have guest clubs (newsletter codes, welcome drinks, Wi-Fi tiers). It takes 2 minutes and pays off.

Pro tip: Late check-out is easiest on Sun–Thu and in business districts on weekends.

4) Combine perks

  • Ask for late check-out and luggage storage
  • Request a room near the gym/spa if you’ll use it
  • If breakfast isn’t included, ask about café partners nearby

5) Fix small issues early

Report noise or maintenance within the first hour. Properties will move you when they can, especially if you’re polite and specific.
